Identify Your Banana

Large banana? Small banana? Green bananas? Yellow bananas? Brown bananas?! Everyone has a banana, but how would you catagorize your banana? Fruit Vision will tell you how ripe your banana is!

Let us tell you how ripe your banana is!!

Stage 1 - Under Ripe

Green

Texture and flavor: very firm, starchy with little flavor or sweetness
Uses: cooked in stews and curries, or fried as a substitute in a recipe that calls for plaintains

Stage 2 - Almost Ripe

Green-ish yellow with green stem

Texture and flavor: firm with mild flavor
Uses: raw, or cooked in stews and curries; mashed and fried as fritters; sauteed for desserts

Stage 3 - Completely Ripe

Yellow

Texture and flavor: tender, but holds shape; delicate, sweet flavor and aroma
Uses: raw in fruit salads, flambeed or sauteed for desserts

Stage 4 - Very Ripe

Yellow with brown spots

Texture and flavor: very tender and sweet with pronounced banana flavor and aroma
Uses: raw in fruit salads and smoothies, flambeed or sauteed in desserts and breads

Stage 5 - Over Ripe

Large brown spots or completely brown

Texture and flavor: very soft and almost sticky; strong flavor and aroma
Uses: mashed into milkshakes, breads, cakes, pancake batter, and smoothies
